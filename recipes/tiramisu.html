Tiramisu

Description

Layered flavours of coffee, cream, and cake; what more could you ask for? Tiramisu's name originates from the Italian phrase tirami su, which means “pick me up”. Trust me when I say that after you give it a taste, you'll also agree that the title is fitting.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 8 ounces mascarpone cheese, room temperature
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups cold espresso
  • 3 tbs coffee liqueur (eg. Khalua)
  • 1 Package Lady Fingers
  • Cocoa powder for dusting on top
