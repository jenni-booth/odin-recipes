Pavlova

Description

Pavlova is a delicious meringue dessert originating from either Australia or New Zealand1. Named after the Russian ballerina Anna Pavlova, this dessert is crisp on the outside but soft and slighty chewy on the inside. Be the talk of the table at any holiday meal by making this easy, yet show-stopping, treat.

Made up of only five ingredients, this is a great recipe for novice and expert bakers alike!

Ingredients

  • 3 Egg whites
  • 6oz (3/4 cup) Berry fine sugar
  • 1 tsp Cornstarch
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp Vinegar
